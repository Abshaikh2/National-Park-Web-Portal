OUR MISSION

We preserve parks today and create park stewards for tomorrow. A national park is an area set aside by a national government for the preservation of the natural environment.We acquire the missing pieces of our national parks, the privately owned land located within and adjacent to our national parks’ boundaries. We also bring thousands of kids from under-served communities to our parks; they are our future caretakers of these priceless resources, “National parks and reserves are an integral aspect of intelligent use of natural resources.
